Security & Privacy

Enterprise-Grade Data Security

Your data is your most valuable asset. We ensure it remains 100% private, secure, and under your absolute control.

On-Premises Security

Data lives entirely on your office machine. Daily backups are uploaded to your personal Google Drive.

  • Complete Local Ownership: No external vendor or server has access to your business records.
  • Encrypted Drive Backups: Automatic backup cycles compile and sync database copies to your secure Google Drive.
  • Physical Isolation: The safest setup for industries requiring strict on-site storage compliance.

Dedicated Cloud Security

Isolated dedicated servers with industry-standard security. Not shared hosting.

  • Dedicated Server Isolation: Your database runs on isolated server resources, completely separate from other clients.
  • Robust Access Control: Protected with corporate-grade firewall rules, SSH keys, and SSL encryption.
  • Regular Security Patches: Proactive updates to ensure protection against security vulnerabilities.
